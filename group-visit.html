Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book my group visit?

We recommend booking at least 4-6 weeks in advance to secure your preferred date and time, especially during peak seasons (April-June and September-November). For larger groups or special requests, 2-3 months advance booking is advised.

What if we need to cancel or change our booking?

Changes and cancellations can be made up to 14 days before your visit date without penalty. For changes or cancellations within 14 days of your visit, a 50% fee may apply. Please contact our group bookings team as soon as possible if you need to make any changes.

Can we bring our own food for lunch?

Outside food and beverages are not permitted in the galleries. However, we have designated areas for school and youth groups to eat packed lunches. These spaces must be booked in advance. We also have cafes and restaurants on-site offering a range of options for groups.

Is there coach parking available?

Yes, there is designated coach drop-off and pick-up area outside the main entrance. Long-term coach parking is available at nearby parking facilities. Please inform us if you will be arriving by coach when making your booking.

Do you offer visits in languages other than English?

Yes, we offer guided tours in Spanish, French, Mandarin, Japanese, and German. These must be booked at least 4 weeks in advance and are subject to guide availability. There is an additional fee of $50 for non-English language tours.
