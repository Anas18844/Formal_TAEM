Key Donation Policies

1- Acceptance of Donations and Sponsorship Policy Each donation or sponsorship is evaluated individually. The Museum cannot accept contributions that are unlawful, could imply complicity with illegal activities, or pose significant risks of harm to the Museum's reputation or operations.
2- Anti-Money Laundering and Refusal of Donations Policy The Museum is committed to preventing financial crimes. It refuses donations that are anonymous (except for collection box contributions), made through unidentified intermediaries, or suspected to be proceeds of illegal activities.
3- Gift Aid Declaration UK taxpayers can enhance their donations through Gift Aid, allowing the Museum to reclaim an additional 25p for every Â£1 donated. This requires donors to complete a declaration form.
4- Legacy Giving Individuals can support the Museum's future by leaving monetary gifts or objects in their wills. Certain donated objects may qualify for tax relief under schemes like Acceptance in Lieu.
