Volunteering

Share your passion, meet new people, and make a difference — become a museum volunteer today.

Why volunteer with us?

Volunteering at our museum offers a unique opportunity to engage with world-class collections and contribute to preserving and sharing cultural heritage. Whether you're looking to develop new skills, meet like-minded people, or simply give back to the community, volunteering with us provides a rewarding experience in a stimulating environment.

Develop new skills

Gain practical experience in a museum setting, developing transferable skills in communication, research, and public engagement.

Meet new people

Join a vibrant community of volunteers from diverse backgrounds who share your passion for history, art, and culture.

Make a difference

Help us engage visitors from around the world and make cultural heritage accessible to diverse audiences.

How to apply

We welcome applications from people of all backgrounds and experiences. Our volunteer recruitment process is designed to match your skills and interests with the right opportunity.

  1. Browse our current opportunities and select the role that interests you
  2. Complete the online application form, telling us about your relevant skills and experience
  3. If your application is successful, we'll invite you for an informal interview
  4. Complete a short induction and role-specific training
  5. Begin your volunteering journey with us!

Frequently asked questions

What skills do I need to volunteer?

Different roles require different skills, but enthusiasm, reliability, and a passion for cultural heritage are important for all our volunteer positions. Specific skills required will be detailed in each role description.

How much time do I need to commit?

This varies by role. Some positions require a regular weekly commitment, while others may be project-based or event-specific. Most roles ask for a minimum commitment period to ensure continuity.

Will I receive training?

Yes, all volunteers receive a general induction about the museum and role-specific training. Ongoing support is provided by staff and experienced volunteers.

Can I claim expenses?

We reimburse reasonable travel expenses and provide a meal allowance for volunteers working a full day. Details will be provided during the application process.

I have access requirements. Can I still volunteer?

Absolutely. We are committed to making volunteering accessible to everyone. Please let us know about any access requirements or adjustments you might need during the application process.
