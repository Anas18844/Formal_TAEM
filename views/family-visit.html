Tips for Your Visit

🕒 Timing Your Visit

Weekday mornings are usually quieter. Consider arriving when we open at 10am for a more relaxed experience.

🍎 Food and Drinks

Our family-friendly cafeteria offers child-friendly options. You can also bring your own snacks to enjoy in designated areas.

🧸 What to Bring

Bring a small backpack with essentials. Strollers are permitted in most gallery spaces.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are there child-friendly restrooms?

Yes, we have family restrooms located on every floor. All restrooms are equipped with changing tables for babies and toddlers.

Is there a place to store our belongings?

Yes, we offer free coat check and bag storage at the main entrance. Strollers can be checked or used throughout most of the museum.

What if my child gets tired during our visit?

We have several quiet seating areas throughout the museum where you can take a break. Our garden courtyard also provides a nice space for children to relax.

Are there any age restrictions for activities?

Most of our family activities are designed for children aged 3-12, but we have specific programs for different age groups. Check the description of each activity for age recommendations.
